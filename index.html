Okotoks Men's Chorus

Who We Are


The Okotoks Men’s Chorus is an exciting, growing group of guys who to have fun and sing - presenting a diverse repertoire including sacred, classic, folk and popular music. An interest in singing is all that is required; no knowledge of music or singing is needed. Come sing with the OMC!

Conductor: Tim Korthuis
Accompanist: Heather Haaland
